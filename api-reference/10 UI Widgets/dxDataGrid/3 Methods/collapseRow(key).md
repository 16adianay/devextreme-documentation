---
##### shortDescription
Allows you to collapse a specific group or master row by its key.

##### param(key): any
The key of the group or master row.

---
To collapse a [group row](/concepts/05%20Widgets/DataGrid/001%20Visual%20Elements/100%20Group%20Rows '/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/DataGrid/Visual_Elements/#Group_Rows'), call this method with an array each member of which is a grouping value. To collapse a [master row](/api-reference/10%20UI%20Widgets/dxDataGrid/1%20Configuration/masterDetail '/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxDataGrid/Configuration/masterDetail/'), pass its key to this method.
