---
##### shortDescription
Cancels the selection of rows with specific keys.

##### param(keys): Array<any>
The row keys.

##### return: Promise<any>
A Promise that is resolved after selection is cleared. It is a [native Promise](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Promise) or a [jQuery.Promise](https://api.jquery.com/Types/#Promise) when you use jQuery.
