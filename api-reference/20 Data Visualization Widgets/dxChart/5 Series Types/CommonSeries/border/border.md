##### shortDescription
Configures the series border (in area-like series) or the series point border (in bar-like and bubble series).

---
Declared in [commonSeriesSettings](/api-reference/20%20Data%20Visualization%20Widgets/dxChart/1%20Configuration/commonSeriesSettings '/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Visualization_Widgets/dxChart/Configuration/commonSeriesSettings/'), the **border** settings apply to all series in the chart. Declared in a [series](/api-reference/20%20Data%20Visualization%20Widgets/dxChart/1%20Configuration/series '/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Visualization_Widgets/dxChart/Configuration/series/') configuration object, the **border** settings apply to this particular series only. The series-specific **border** settings override the common ones.
